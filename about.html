My Developer Story

     Hello My name is Grace and this is my developer story. From a very early age I have always been fascinated with computers.
In the begining I was intrested in the hardwear of computers and how they functioned. However, in fifth grade I learned what
computer softwear was, and that became the new part of computers that I was facinated with. My computer teacher at the time
used the web site code.org, to teach my class the basics of coding. Where you would select directions on the computer and
they would be executed by an animation on the screen. I did something similar in my middle school STEM class, where my group
and I programmed directions for a maze into a computer, and the directions were excuted by a Lego car robot. Looking back now these
actvities are very similar to computer programming, where the raw code is the instructions is what executes the instructions (code).
Although this is a simplifid version of what computer programming is, I think having this basic understanding will help in
these early stages of learning how code.
